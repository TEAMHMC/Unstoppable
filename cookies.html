Cookie Policy

Effective Date: June 8, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 8, 2026  ·  Version 1.0
The short version. The Unstoppable site uses only essential cookies and local browser storage needed to make the page work. We do not use cookies for cross-site advertising. We do not sell information about your browsing.

01 What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are used to make sites work, to remember preferences, and sometimes to gather statistics. Local storage is a related browser feature that lets a site save information on your device without using a cookie.

This page describes both cookies and local storage used by the Unstoppable site.

02 What We Use

Name Type Purpose Duration
unstoppable-consent Local storage Remembers that you have seen and dismissed the consent or info banner so it does not reappear on every page load. Until cleared
session Session cookie Set by the hosting provider when you load the page. Used to keep the request consistent during a single visit. End of browser session
analytics (aggregate) First-party analytics Aggregated page-view counts used to understand which parts of the program get the most attention. No individual visitor is identified. Up to 26 months

03 What We Do Not Use

  • We do not use third-party advertising cookies.
  • We do not use cookies that follow you across other websites.
  • We do not sell or share information collected through cookies with data brokers.

04 Third-Party Embeds

Some sections of the Unstoppable site embed content from third parties, such as YouTube videos. When you press play on an embedded video, the third party may set its own cookies subject to its own privacy and cookie policies. We do not control those cookies.

  • YouTube: See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Eventbrite (if linked): See Eventbrite Privacy.

05 How to Control Cookies

You can control or clear cookies and local storage at any time using your browser's settings. Most browsers let you:

  • See what cookies a site has set
  • Delete cookies for a single site or all sites
  • Block cookies before they are set
  • Browse in a private or incognito window that clears cookies when closed

Blocking essential cookies or local storage may cause parts of the site (for example, the consent banner) to display on every visit.

06 Do Not Track

We honor the spirit of Do Not Track. We do not use cross-site tracking or advertising cookies, regardless of the Do Not Track header your browser sends.

07 Updates

If we add a new cookie or change a purpose, we will update this page with a new effective date. Material changes will also be noted in the Privacy Policy.

01 About Health Matters Clinic

Health Matters Clinic ("HMC," "we," "us," or "our") is a California 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in Los Angeles, California. HMC advances health equity by connecting underserved communities to free and low-cost health services, wellness education, volunteer-powered outreach, and community-centered care programming.

HMC operates under the laws of the State of California. Our principal place of operations is Los Angeles County, California.

As a nonprofit, HMC is mission-driven. Our platforms exist to serve the community, coordinate volunteers, and support partner organizations. We do not sell personal data, and we are committed to transparency, dignity, and trust in every interaction.

09 CalmKit and Digital Wellness Tools

9.1 Nature of CalmKit

CalmKit (calmkit.healthmatters.clinic) is a free, community-facing digital wellness coaching tool that provides guided breathing exercises, self-reflection prompts, and wellness coaching content. CalmKit is not a mental health treatment platform, is not operated by licensed therapists or psychologists in a clinical capacity through this tool, and does not create a therapeutic relationship.

9.2 Self-Screening Responses

Any self-screening or self-assessment responses you enter into CalmKit are processed locally in your browser session and are not stored, transmitted to HMC's servers, or shared with any third party. CalmKit is designed to be an anonymous, session-based experience. See our Privacy Policy for technical details.

9.3 Not a Crisis Resource

CalmKit is not designed for crisis intervention. If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, suicidal thoughts, or a psychiatric crisis, please contact 988 (Suicide and Crisis Lifeline), text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line), call 911, or go to your nearest emergency room. Do not use CalmKit as a substitute for crisis support.

9.4 Wellness Content Disclaimer

CalmKit content, including coaching prompts, breathing exercises, and wellness guidance, is evidence-informed but does not constitute clinical advice. Participation in CalmKit activities is entirely voluntary. You are encouraged to consult with a licensed mental health professional for any clinical concerns.
